About Me


I'm Manya Kapoor, a passionate and driven second-year engineering student majoring in Instrumentation and Control with a minor in Computer Science at NIT Jalandhar. I thrive on academic excellence while actively exploring the world of tech and design.


My journey blends creativity with logic — I specialize in UI/UX design using Figma, and I’m actively building my skills in web development through hands-on projects and cohort-based learning. I’ve also worked with React Native, Supabase, and AI tools to build functional, aesthetic productivity apps.


I’m passionate about community building and collaboration. As a member of the Google Developer Student Club (GDSC), I engage with peers to foster innovation and share knowledge. My role in the Literary & Debating Club has honed my public speaking and critical thinking skills, allowing me to express ideas effectively.


Outside of tech, I enjoy food, fitness (hello gym time!), and music. I'm always eager to learn, build cool stuff, and contribute to communities that make a difference.
